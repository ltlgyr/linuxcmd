1.安装 bluez-util 

2.配置文件：
/etc/bluetooth/rfcomm.conf

3.命令
1>hcid
2>hciemu
激活设备
3>hciconfig hci0 up

4>hcidump
5>hcitool
	hcitool scan
	hcitool cc 

6>hciattach

7> rfcomm create dev
	mknod /dev/rfcomm0 c 216 1
	chmod 666 /dev/rfcomm0
	rfcomm bind /dev/rfcomm0 00:21:19:A4:E0:F1 1

8> rfcomm bind /dev/rfcomm0  蓝牙地址

停止设备
	rfkill block 0
	rfkill block all
